Top CBSE Schools in Bangalore, 2026

Published on: January 31, 2026

Bangalore has some of India’s most sought-after CBSE schools, known for strong academics, disciplined environments, and good exposure to Olympiads, competitive exams, and co-curricular activities.

Parents can choose from legacy brands like National Public School, Delhi Public School, Sri Kumaran, and New Horizon Gurukul to newer campuses with modern infrastructure spread across North, South, East, West, and Central Bangalore.

Popular CBSE Schools in Bangalore (2026)

The city’s popular CBSE schools combine strong board results with well-planned campuses, digital classrooms, and a focus on holistic development through sports, arts, and clubs.

Some well-known names across the city include National Public School (various branches), Delhi Public School, New Horizon Gurukul, CMR National Public School, Orchids The International School, BGS International Residential School, Navkis Educational Centre, TISB, and Inventure Academy.

Fees of Top CBSE Schools in Bangalore

CBSE school fees in Bangalore vary widely depending on brand, location, and facilities, ranging from around ₹30,000 per year in some traditional schools to ₹2–3 lakh or more in premium campuses.

Central and older neighbourhoods often show the widest fee spread, while newer IT corridor schools on Sarjapur Road, Whitefield, and Electronic City typically fall in the mid-to-premium bracket.

Infrastructure & Facilities

Most top CBSE schools in Bangalore offer smart classrooms, well-equipped science and computer labs, libraries, and multi-sport grounds, with many also providing swimming pools, auditoriums, and specialised activity rooms.

Premium campuses around Sarjapur, Whitefield, Electronic City, and North Bangalore focus on large, green layouts with separate junior–senior blocks, indoor sports complexes, and STEM/robotics labs to support activity-based learning.

CBSE Boarding & Residential Schools in Bangalore

For families seeking residential options, Bangalore has several CBSE boarding schools that combine hostel facilities with structured academics and a strong sports and co-curricular ecosystem.

Popular CBSE residential or semi-residential schools include BGS International Residential School, some Gurukul-style campuses, and other outskirts schools that offer weekly or full boarding with transport connectivity to the city.

Admissions & Selection Criteria

Most CBSE schools in Bangalore open admissions between September and January for the next academic year, with registration forms available online through school or aggregator websites.

Selection commonly involves basic interaction or assessment for KG–primary, written tests for higher classes, previous report cards, and preference for siblings or alumni, especially in high-demand campuses like NPS, DPS, and Sri Kumaran.

CBSE Results & Academic Performance

Top CBSE schools in Bangalore regularly report high pass percentages and a strong share of students scoring 90% and above in Classes 10 and 12 board exams.

Schools like NPS group, Delhi Public School branches, Sri Kumaran, and New Horizon Gurukul are frequently cited for consistent CBSE results and good representation in competitive exams, Olympiads, and entrance tests.

FAQ – Top CBSE Schools in Bangalore

Which is the number one CBSE school in Bangalore?

There is no single official “number one” CBSE school in Bangalore, but National Public School (especially NPS Indiranagar), Delhi Public School campuses, and Sri Kumaran Schools are consistently rated among the top by parents and school-ranking portals.

Which school is ranked 1 in Bangalore?

Different surveys rank schools differently each year; however, NPS group schools, DPS Bangalore, Sri Kumaran, and a few premium campuses in Sarjapur–Whitefield often appear in the top positions for CBSE curriculum in Bangalore.

Which school is top in the CBSE result?

Schools like National Public School (various branches), Delhi Public School, Sri Kumaran, and New Horizon Gurukul frequently report high CBSE board scores with many students scoring above 90% in Classes 10 and 12, keeping them among the top performers in Bangalore.

What is the fees of NPS Indiranagar in Bangalore?

As per recent publicly available data, the indicative first-year fee for primary classes at NPS Indiranagar (including tuition and one-time charges) is in the range of about ₹1.75–1.85 lakh, and may be revised periodically. Parents should always confirm the latest fee structure directly with the school office or official website.

How do I choose the best CBSE school in Bangalore for my child?

Compare factors like distance from home, fee budget, board results, class strength, teacher quality, infrastructure, transport, and feedback from existing parents. It is also helpful to visit the campus, attend school orientations, and understand the school’s approach to discipline, homework, and co-curricular activities before finalising.
